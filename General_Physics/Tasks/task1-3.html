习题3：复摆周期

题目：一质量 $m$ 、长 $L$ 的均匀细杆绕端点作小角度摆动，证明其周期为：

  1. $T = 2\pi\sqrt{\frac{2L}{3g}}$

解答：

\begin{aligned} \textcolor{#11cc98}{\text{转动惯量}} &\quad I = \frac{1}{3}mL^2 \\ \textcolor{#11cc98}{\text{回复力矩}} &\quad \tau = -mg\frac{L}{2}\sin\theta \approx -mg\frac{L}{2}\theta \\ \textcolor{#11cc98}{\text{转动定律}} &\quad I\alpha = \tau \Rightarrow \frac{d^2\theta}{dt^2} + \frac{3g}{2L}\theta = 0 \\ \textcolor{#11cc98}{\text{周期公式}} &\quad T = 2\pi\sqrt{\frac{I}{mgr\_{\text{CM}}}} = 2\pi\sqrt{\frac{2L}{3g}} \end{aligned}
