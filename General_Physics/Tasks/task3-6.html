习题6：四维动量

题目：一个粒子的四维动量为 $\mathbf{P} = (E/c, p_x, p_y, p_z)$，其中 $E = 5\ \text{GeV}$，$p_x = 3\ \text{GeV}/c$，$p_y = 2\ \text{GeV}/c$，$p_z = 0$。求：

  1. 粒子的静止质量 $m_0$
  2. 粒子的速度 $v$

解答：

\begin{aligned} \textcolor{#11cc98}{\text{四维动量不变量}} &\quad \mathbf{P} \cdot \mathbf{P} = \left( \frac{E}{c} \right)^2 - p^2 = (m_0 c)^2 \\ p^2 &= p_x^2 + p_y^2 + p_z^2 = (3\ \text{GeV}/c)^2 + (2\ \text{GeV}/c)^2 + 0 = 13\ (\text{GeV}/c)^2 \\ \textcolor{#11cc98}{\text{1.}} \quad (m_0 c^2)^2 &= E^2 - (pc)^2 = (5\ \text{GeV})^2 - (13\ \text{GeV}^2) = 12\ \text{GeV}^2 \\ m_0 c^2 &= \sqrt{12}\ \text{GeV} \approx 3.464\ \text{GeV} \\ m_0 &= \frac{3.464\ \text{GeV}}{c^2} \\ \textcolor{#11cc98}{\text{相对论能量公式}} &\quad E = \gamma m_0 c^2 \implies \gamma = \frac{E}{m_0 c^2} = \frac{5}{3.464} \approx 1.443 \\ \textcolor{#11cc98}{\text{2.}} \quad \gamma &= \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - v^2/c^2}} \implies 1 - v^2/c^2 = \frac{1}{\gamma^2} \approx \frac{1}{2.083} \approx 0.48 \\ v^2/c^2 &\approx 0.52 \implies v \approx c \sqrt{0.52} \approx 0.72c \end{aligned}
